Principles

Triage: fix things when they become the most pressing opportunity to move forward.
  • Progress over polish: publish early, refine when it meaningfully unlocks the next step.
  • Reality first: engineering constraints (dust, cold, vacuum) drive plans.
  • Plain language: drafts readable by smart teenagers beat legalese.
  • Property & consent: no tampering without permission; contracts clear and auditable.
  • Transparency: decisions and changes documented in public pages/issues.

How We Use This

When choosing what to do next, we ask: which change removes the biggest blocker to real progress? That’s what we do first.
